About Us

**Origins and Mission:** DedSec was initially formed as a response to the misuse of technology and the erosion of privacy in the game's fictional universe. The group's members are hacktivists who believe in using their skills to fight against the abuse of surveillance, data manipulation, and other cyber threats. DedSec's primary goal is to empower the public by exposing the truth and holding those in power accountable for their actions. **Attire:** DedSec members are characterized by their unconventional and edgy attire, which reflects their rebellious nature and hacker subculture. Individual DedSec members customize their attire to express their unique personalities, and the overall look is meant to be a counter-culture statement against the conformity and surveillance culture depicted in the game.
